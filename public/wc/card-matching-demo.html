Card Matching Game

How to Play:

  • Blue pins on the left should match with orange pins on the right
  • Drag a blue card onto an orange card to connect them
  • Click on the connection dot (middle of the line) to disconnect cards
  • When cards are connected, dragging one moves both
  • Click "Check" to verify your connections
  • Correct connections will disappear, incorrect ones stay
